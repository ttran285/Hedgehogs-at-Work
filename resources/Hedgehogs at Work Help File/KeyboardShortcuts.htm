Here are the Keyboard Shortcuts used throughout the game.

Main Menu:

Play Button: Press '1'

Instructions: Press '2'

High Scores: Press '3'

Exit: Press 'Esc'

CHM File: Press 'F1'

Sound: Press 'F2'

Level Select:

Level One: Press '1'

Level Two: Press '2'

Level Three: Press '3'

Back to Main Menu: Press 'Backspace'

Instructions:

Back to Main Menu: Press 'Backspace'

Next Slide: Press 'Right Arrow Key'

Previous Slide: Press 'Left Arrow Key'

High Scores:

Back to Main Menu: Press 'Backspace'

Print: Press 'p'

Level Complete:

Continue: Press 'Right Arrow Key'

Certificate:

Continue: Press 'Right Arrow Key'

Print: Press 'p'

Gameplay Activities:

Pause Button: Press 'p'

Pause Button Menu:

Resume: Press 'Right Arrow Key'

Return to Main Menu: Press 'Backspace'
